Assumptions and Limitations ▼
  • Coordinates must be in a projected coordinate system (meters). Geographic coordinates (latitude/longitude) are not supported.
  • Traverse closure assumes points are entered in sequential order along the traverse path.
  • Outlier detection uses a 3-sigma threshold from centroid; best suited for clustered detail points rather than linear traverses.
  • Duplicate threshold is 1mm; near-duplicate threshold is 1cm.
  • Traverse precision ratings: Good at 1:10000 or better, Acceptable at 1:5000 or better.
